About

Mission

We have always gone back to programming as the way to bring our dreams into reality. Everybody here at StackHack understands the demand for programming in the future as well as the plethora of problems that it will solve in society. We strive to accomplish three things in class:

  • Real-World Application
  • Healthy Classroom Climate
  • Interactive Lessons

As a result of this, we took it incubumbent upon ourselves to provide the same tools that will better our society in a streamline way for everybody. StackHack hopes to light the fire underneath everybody passionate about making the world a better place, and give them the tools to do so. We hope that the courses that we provide will invigoruate our students to learn more about coding and the effect that it has on the world. After learning these skills, the sky is the limit for our students.
